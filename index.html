About Me
Profile

I am a translational oncology scientist who uses cell-based and molecular approaches to understand how transcriptional CDKs and inflammatory signaling shape tumor behaviour and treatment response. My background spans cancer biology, degrader pharmacology, and quantitative analysis, with a consistent focus on connecting target biology to clear hypotheses for new therapies.

In the lab, I like to build robust, modular workflows rather than one-off experiments—designing dose–response and time-course studies with multi-parameter readouts, then using R and Python to turn noisy data into decision-ready summaries for cross-functional project teams. I am comfortable moving between the bench and the notebook, translating experimental results into next-step designs for drug discovery and translational research.

My Work
Selected Project

Project: Selective degradation of oncogenic kinases using PROTAC-style small molecules targeting transcriptional CDKs.

Target Validation
  • Phospho-specific and quantitative immunoblotting plus qPCR and ELISA to confirm on-target modulation of transcriptional CDKs, NF-κB, and cytokine-related signaling at both protein and mRNA levels.
  • HPG protein synthesis and related metabolic assays to measure how target perturbation rewires biosynthetic activity and cellular fitness in tumor and immune-relevant models.
  • Immunostaining and proximity ligation assays to profile protein interaction, pathway activation, and immune-relevant responses that link target engagement to functional phenotypes.
Preclinical Candidate Evaluation
  • Design and execute cell-based screening campaigns and dose- and time-course studies across prostate, breast, leukemia, and immune-relevant models to profile panels of small-molecule inhibitors and degraders.
  • Quantify viability, proliferation, cytotoxicity, and apoptosis using multi-parameter flow cytometry, live-cell imaging (e.g., Incucyte), and plate-based assays to support hit triage, lead ranking, and risk assessment.
  • Fit EC50, IC50, and DC50 curves and integrate basic exposure–response/PK/PD analysis to compare potency, selectivity, and liabilities, informing prioritization of preclinical candidates.
Translational Oncology
  • Plan and interpret RNA-seq and quantitative proteomics to define treatment-induced pathway and gene-expression signatures in cancer and inflammatory models.
  • Integrate in vitro pharmacology data with public tumor and survival datasets to generate target, indication, and biomarker hypotheses that support portfolio and indication strategy.
  • Select disease-relevant in vitro models and pharmacodynamic readouts aligned with transcriptional CDK, NF-κB, and cytokine biology to connect mechanistic findings to preclinical design and life-cycle management questions.
Technical Skills & Methods
Core Toolkit
Gene Editing & Functional Genomics
  • Design and execution of CRISPR KO/KI strategies and small-scale screens in human cancer cell models.
  • shRNA/siRNA and lentiviral delivery to modulate gene expression and validate targets.
Mammalian Cell Culture & Cell-Based Pharmacology
  • Long-term maintenance, expansion, and banking of diverse human cancer and immune-relevant cell lines (e.g., LNCaP, 293T, THP-1) with routine mycoplasma testing and cryopreservation.
  • Design of plate-based and imaging assays to quantify treatment effects on cell health, proliferation, and death across panels of inhibitors and degraders.
  • Hands-on experience with multi-parameter flow cytometry, FACS, and kinetic live-cell imaging platforms (e.g., Incucyte).
Molecular Biology Assays
  • Cloning, plasmid preparation, and high-quality DNA/RNA extraction from mammalian cells and tissues.
  • PCR, RT-PCR, and qRT-PCR assay development, optimization, and data interpretation.
  • Hybridization Chain Reaction fluorescence in situ hybridization (HCR-FISH) and related nucleic-acid detection workflows.
Protein & Antibody-Based Assays
  • SDS-PAGE and quantitative / phospho-specific immunoblotting for signaling and target-engagement analysis.
  • Immunoprecipitation / co-immunoprecipitation, ELISA, and cytokine profiling to link pathway modulation to functional responses.
  • HPG-based protein synthesis measurements, Coomassie and Ponceau S staining, and small-scale recombinant protein expression in HEK293.
Data Analysis, Biostatistics & Bioinformatics
  • Routine use of GraphPad Prism, FlowJo, Incucyte analysis software, Image Lab, confocal imaging tools, and LabArchives ELN.
  • R and Python on Unix/Linux systems for QC, statistics, visualization, and basic transcriptomic / proteomic data interpretation.
  • Development of tested, LLM-assisted analytics pipelines to standardize normalization, QC, and reporting across projects.
Project, Models & Compliance
  • Cross-functional project coordination and scientific communication with chemistry, bioinformatics, and clinical collaborators.
  • Experience with in vivo / ex vivo model systems including Drosophila melanogaster and Mus musculus-based workflows.
  • Familiarity with cGMP, ICH-GCP, and GLP expectations; emphasis on reproducible, well-documented experimental design and execution.
