About Me
Profile

I am a translational oncology scientist focused on integrating mechanistic biology, quantitative cell-based pharmacology, and data-driven analysis to understand how transcriptional CDKs, NF-κB–linked inflammatory signaling, and cytokine pathways shape tumor behavior. I routinely design and execute high-content experiments using multi-parameter flow cytometry, live-cell imaging, and CRISPR/RNAi perturbations to quantify viability, proliferation, apoptosis, cytotoxicity, and pathway activation under small-molecule inhibitor and degrader treatment.

I enjoy building robust, reproducible assay workflows and analysis pipelines in R and Python, connecting exposure–response relationships, signaling readouts, and omics data to clear hypotheses and next-step experiments. I’m comfortable operating in cross-functional discovery teams and translating complex datasets into decision-ready insights that support hit triage, lead optimization, and indication strategy for novel cancer therapeutics.

My Work
Selected Project

Project: Selective degradation of oncogenic kinases using PROTAC-style small molecules targeting transcriptional CDKs.

Target Validation
  • Phospho-specific and quantitative immunoblotting plus qPCR and ELISA to confirm on-target modulation of transcriptional CDKs, NF-κB, and cytokine-related signaling at both protein and mRNA levels.
  • HPG protein synthesis and related metabolic assays to measure how target perturbation rewires biosynthetic activity and cellular fitness in tumor and immune-relevant models.
  • Immunostaining and proximity ligation assays to profile protein interaction, pathway activation, and immune-relevant responses that link target engagement to functional phenotypes.
Preclinical Candidate Evaluation
  • Design and execute cell-based screening campaigns and dose- and time-course studies across prostate, breast, leukemia, and immune-relevant models to profile panels of small-molecule inhibitors and degraders.
  • Quantify viability, proliferation, cytotoxicity, and apoptosis using multi-parameter flow cytometry, live-cell imaging (e.g., Incucyte), and plate-based assays to support hit triage, lead ranking, and risk assessment.
  • Fit EC50, IC50, and DC50 curves and integrate basic exposure–response/PK/PD analysis to compare potency, selectivity, and liabilities, informing prioritization of preclinical candidates.
Translational Oncology
  • Plan and interpret RNA-seq and quantitative proteomics to define treatment-induced pathway and gene-expression signatures in cancer and inflammatory models.
  • Integrate in vitro pharmacology data with public tumor and survival datasets to generate target, indication, and biomarker hypotheses that support portfolio and indication strategy.
  • Select disease-relevant in vitro models and pharmacodynamic readouts aligned with transcriptional CDK, NF-κB, and cytokine biology to connect mechanistic findings to preclinical design and life-cycle management questions.
Technical Skills & Methods
Core Toolkit
Gene Editing & Functional Genomics
  • CRISPR KO/KI and screening workflows in human cancer cell models.
  • RNAi (shRNA/siRNA) and lentiviral transduction for target perturbation.
Mammalian Cell Culture & Cell-Based Pharmacology
  • Routine culture of human cancer and immune-relevant cell lines (e.g., LNCaP, 293T, THP-1) with seeding, passaging, and routine mycoplasma testing, including cryopreservation.
  • Cell viability, proliferation, cytotoxicity, cell-cycle, and apoptosis assays to characterize panels of inhibitors and degraders.
  • Multi-parameter flow cytometry and fluorescence-activated cell sorting (FACS); live-cell imaging (e.g., Incucyte) for kinetic readouts.
Molecular Biology Assays
  • Cloning, plasmid preparation, and DNA/RNA extraction.
  • PCR, RT-PCR, and qRT-PCR assay development and optimization.
  • Hybridization Chain Reaction fluorescence in situ hybridization (HCR-FISH) and related nucleic-acid detection methods.
Protein & Antibody-Based Assays
  • Protein gel electrophoresis, quantitative and phospho-specific immunoblotting for signaling and target-engagement analysis.
  • Immunoprecipitation / co-immunoprecipitation, ELISA, and cytokine profiling to connect pathway modulation to functional responses.
  • HPG protein synthesis assays, Coomassie and Ponceau S staining, and recombinant protein expression in HEK293.
Data Analysis, Biostatistics & Bioinformatics
  • GraphPad Prism, FlowJo, Incucyte analysis software, Image Lab, confocal imaging tools, and LabArchives ELN.
  • R and Python on Unix/Linux systems for omics analysis, QC, statistics, and visualization.
  • LLM-assisted, tested analytics pipelines for normalization, QC, and automation to support reproducible decision-making.
