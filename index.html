My Work
Selected Project

Project: Selective degradation of oncogenic kinases using PROTAC-style small molecules targeting transcriptional CDKs.

Target Validation
  • Quantitative immunoblotting and qPCR to confirm target engagement and pathway modulation.
  • Metabolic response assays (e.g., HPG incorporation).
  • Immune-response detection by ELISA, immunostaining, and proximity ligation assays.
Preclinical Candidate Evaluation
  • Sscratch wound healing assays to quantify cell migration.
  • Dose–response viability profiling with EC50/IC50/DC50 fitting.
  • Cell-cycle and apoptosis characterization by flow cytometry.
Translational Oncology
  • RNA-seq and proteomics to map pathway-level responses to treatment.
  • UMAP and volcano plots to visualize molecular changes.
  • Survival analyses to link molecular signatures with patient outcomes.
